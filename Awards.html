Young Scientist Award of the Physical Society of Japan

Issued by:The Physical Society of Japan, Apr 2024

Info: Link

Every year, the Physical Society of Japan presents its Young Scientist Awards to young researchers to recognize outstanding achievements in their early research careers. This year’s winners were recently decided by the board of directors of the JPS based on the recommendations of the selection committees established in 19 divisions of the JPS.

Kohsuke Yagi Quark Matter Award

Issued by: RIKEN iTHEMS, Nov 2023

Info: Link

Awarded to a young speaker who has given a plenary (invited) talk at Quark Matter: International Conference on Ultra-relativistic Nucleus-Nucleus Collisions, the world's largest international conference in high-energy nuclear physics, the largest international conference in nuclear physics.

Best student presentation award at JPS autumn meeting 2022

Issued by: The Physical Society of Japan, Sep 2022

Info: Link

Award for outstanding oral presentations by students (master's and doctoral) and undergraduate students at the Japanese Physical Society autumn meeting.

Nuclear Physics A Elsevier Young Scientist Awards

Issued by: Elsevier Nuclear Physics A, Nov 2019

Info: Link to the PDF

The best theory talk at the 28th International Conference on Ultra-relativistic Nucleus Nucleus Collisions (Quark Matter 2019). Awarded to promote the work of young, up-and-coming nuclear and high energy physicists.

Global Award for Young Women Researchers

Issued by: Sophia University, Mar 2019

Info: Link

Award for female students at Sophia University's Graduate School of Science and Engineering who have already achieved research results at international conferences, papers, etc., and who show promise for future success.
